FieldFlow turns a Google Sheet into a **searchable, filterable web directory** that you can embed on any website. No coding required.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Who it's for" icon="users">
    Nonprofits, schools, and community organizations that already keep lists in Google Sheets—resource directories, member lists, event catalogs—and want to publish them on their site.
  </Card>
  <Card title="How it works" icon="refresh-cw">
    You connect a Sheet, map columns to fields, and sync. FieldFlow stores the data and serves it to your live directory and embeds. When you edit the Sheet and sync again, the directory updates.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What you get

- **Live directory** — A public page with search, filters, and cards (and optional map view) driven by your Sheet data.
- **Embed anywhere** — A small script you paste into your website so the directory appears in your own pages (WordPress, Wix, custom HTML, etc.).
- **Optional quiz** — A short questionnaire that narrows results by filters, then shows the directory or sends visitors to a filtered URL.

You manage everything from the FieldFlow dashboard: connect Google, create projects, map columns, design the card layout, sync, and copy the embed code. Your team can be invited to the same organization so multiple people can manage projects and syncs.
