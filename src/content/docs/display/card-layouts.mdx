The layout builder lets you design how each record looks in your directory — no coding required. You pick the card style, choose which fields to show, and see a live preview as you go.

## Open the layout builder

Go to your project's **Configure > Display** tab.

## Card styles

FieldFlow offers three card variants:

### Modern (default)
Larger cards with prominent images and generous spacing. Best for directories where visual appeal matters — resource directories, business listings, program catalogs.

### Compact
Smaller, denser cards that show more records on screen. Best for long lists where users want to scan quickly — staff directories, job boards, event calendars.

### Detailed
Full-width cards with extra field visibility. Best when each record has lots of metadata — service directories with hours, eligibility, contact info, and descriptions.

## Choosing which fields to display

Not every field needs to show on the card. In the layout builder:

1. **Title field** — the main heading on each card (usually "Name" or "Organization")
2. **Subtitle** — a secondary line (optional)
3. **Description** — a longer text field shown below the title
4. **Image** — displayed as the card's hero image or thumbnail
5. **Additional fields** — toggle any mapped field on or off

Users can also show/hide optional fields themselves using the **field visibility menu** in the directory toolbar.

## Colors and styling

You can customize:
- **Primary color** — used for links, buttons, and accents
- **Card borders and shadows** — subtle or prominent
- **Header style** — gradient or flat

For more detail, see [Colors & Branding](/display/colors-and-branding/).

## Live preview

Changes update in real-time in the preview panel. What you see is what your visitors will see in the embedded directory.

## Saving

Click **Save** to apply your layout changes. The next time someone loads your directory (or refreshes), they'll see the updated design.
