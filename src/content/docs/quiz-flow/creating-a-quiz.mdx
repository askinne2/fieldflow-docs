## Open the quiz builder

1. Go to your project's **Configure > Quiz** tab
2. Click **Create Quiz** (or edit an existing one)

## Quiz structure

A quiz consists of:

### Title and subtitle
Displayed at the top of the quiz. Keep it clear and action-oriented:
- Good: "Find the right resources for you"
- Avoid: "Resource Finder Quiz Assessment Tool"

### Questions
Each question has:
- **Question text** — what the visitor sees
- **Answer options** — clickable choices (buttons)
- **Tags** — keywords attached to each answer option (used for matching)

### Example question

> **What type of support are you looking for?**
>
> - Housing assistance → tags: `housing`, `shelter`
> - Food & nutrition → tags: `food`, `nutrition`, `pantry`
> - Employment help → tags: `jobs`, `employment`, `career`
> - Legal services → tags: `legal`, `attorney`, `rights`

## Adding questions

1. Click **Add Question**
2. Type the question text
3. Add answer options — each option gets a label and one or more tags
4. Repeat for additional questions

## Tips for writing good questions

- **Keep it short** — 3-5 questions max. More than that and visitors drop off
- **Use plain language** — avoid jargon your audience might not know
- **Make options clear** — each option should be distinct, not overlapping
- **Cover common needs** — think about the top 5-6 reasons people visit your directory
- **Include a catch-all** — add an option like "I'm not sure" or "Something else" that doesn't filter too narrowly

## AI-powered quiz generation

If you provide an **AI Context** description in your project's Overview tab, FieldFlow can generate a complete quiz structure for you. Describe your directory and audience, and the AI creates questions, options, and tags that match your data.

## Activating the quiz

After creating your quiz:
1. Toggle the quiz to **Active** in the quiz builder
2. The quiz URL appears in the **Publish** tab
3. Update your embed code to use `mode: "quiz"` or `mode: "quiz+directory"`
