Quiz Flow is a guided questionnaire that helps visitors find the right resources in your directory. Instead of browsing and filtering manually, visitors answer a few questions and FieldFlow shows them the most relevant results.

## How it works

1. You create a quiz with questions and answer options
2. Each answer is tagged with keywords
3. FieldFlow's AI maps those tags to your directory's taxonomy terms
4. When a visitor completes the quiz, they see a filtered view of your directory matching their answers

## When to use Quiz Flow

Quiz Flow is especially helpful when:
- Your directory has many records and visitors might feel overwhelmed
- Visitors don't know the right terminology to search or filter
- You want to guide people through a decision process (e.g., "What kind of help do you need?")
- Your audience is less tech-savvy and benefits from a conversational approach

## Example

A nonprofit resource directory might have a quiz like:

> **What do you need help with today?**
> - Finding housing
> - Getting food assistance
> - Legal help
> - Finding a job

Based on the answer, the directory filters to show only relevant resources.

## Quiz modes

You can embed the quiz in three ways:

| Mode | What happens |
|---|---|
| **Quiz only** | Shows the quiz, then filtered results |
| **Quiz + Directory** | Shows quiz first, then reveals directory with results. Visitor can also browse the full directory |
| **Directory only** | No quiz, just the standard directory (default) |

Set the mode in your [embed code](/publishing/embed-code/) or the Publish tab.

## Getting started

1. [Create a quiz](/quiz-flow/creating-a-quiz/) with questions and options
2. [Set up tag mapping](/quiz-flow/tag-mapping/) to connect answers to your data
3. Activate the quiz in your project settings
4. Update your embed code to use quiz mode
