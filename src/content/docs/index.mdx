## What is FieldFlow?

FieldFlow connects to your Google Sheets data and transforms it into a live, searchable directory that you embed on any website. Your team keeps editing the spreadsheet they already know — FieldFlow handles the rest.

<CardGrid stagger>
	<Card title="Connect a Google Sheet" icon="document">
		Link your existing spreadsheet. FieldFlow reads your columns and rows automatically.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Map your fields" icon="setting">
		Tell FieldFlow which columns are names, images, phone numbers, addresses, and more. It auto-detects most types.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Design the layout" icon="pencil">
		Choose card styles, colors, and which fields to show. No code — just pick and preview.
	</Card>
	<Card title="Embed anywhere" icon="rocket">
		Copy a snippet into WordPress, Webflow, Squarespace, or any HTML page. Your directory is live.
	</Card>
</CardGrid>

## Who is FieldFlow for?

FieldFlow is built for nonprofits, schools, churches, and community organizations that already track data in Google Sheets and need a way to share it publicly.

**Common use cases:**
- Resource directories (food banks, housing, legal aid)
- Staff or volunteer directories
- Program catalogs
- Event listings
- Business directories
- Job boards
