The Sync tab shows a history of all syncs for your project — both manual and automatic.

## Reading the sync log

Each entry shows:

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| **Time** | When the sync started and completed |
| **Status** | Completed, Failed, Processing, or Queued |
| **Type** | Manual (you clicked Sync Now) or Scheduled (automatic) |
| **Records** | Total processed, with breakdown of created/updated/deleted |
| **Warnings** | Row limit reached, missing fields, or other notes |

## Status meanings

| Status | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Completed** | Sync finished successfully |
| **Processing** | Sync is currently running |
| **Queued** | Sync is waiting to run (another sync may be in progress) |
| **Failed** | Sync encountered an error |

## Troubleshooting failed syncs

### "Google authentication expired"
Your Google connection needs to be refreshed. Go to the **Overview** tab, disconnect, and reconnect your Google account.

### "Sheet not found"
The Google Sheet ID in your project settings may be wrong, or the sheet was deleted/moved. Verify the Sheet ID and worksheet name.

### "Row limit exceeded"
Your plan has a maximum number of rows. The sync processed up to your limit and stopped. Upgrade your plan or reduce rows in your spreadsheet.

### "Rate limit exceeded"
Too many syncs in a short period. Wait a few minutes and try again.

### General failures
Check that:
- Your Google account is still connected
- The spreadsheet still exists and is accessible
- The worksheet name matches a tab in the spreadsheet
- Your internet connection is stable

## Sync log retention

FieldFlow keeps the 10 most recent sync entries per project. Older entries are automatically removed.
