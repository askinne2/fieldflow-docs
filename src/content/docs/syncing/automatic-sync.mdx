---
title: Automatic Sync
description: Schedule regular data updates from your spreadsheet.
---

Automatic sync keeps your directory up to date without manual intervention. Set a schedule and FieldFlow will pull fresh data from your Google Sheet at regular intervals.

## Set up automatic sync

1. Go to your project's **Sync** tab
2. Toggle **Automatic Sync** to on
3. Choose an interval:

| Interval | Best for |
|---|---|
| **Every 30 minutes** | Data that changes frequently throughout the day |
| **Every hour** | Active directories with regular updates |
| **Every 6 hours** | Directories updated a few times per day |
| **Every 12 hours** | Directories updated once or twice daily |
| **Every 24 hours** | Directories updated daily or less |

4. Click **Save**

## How it works

- FieldFlow checks the time since your last successful sync
- When the interval has elapsed, it automatically queues a sync job
- The sync runs in the background on the FieldFlow worker
- If the sync fails, it retries up to 3 times
- You'll receive an email notification if all retries fail (if email notifications are enabled)

## Requirements

Automatic sync requires:
- A connected Google account
- A valid project with at least one successful manual sync

## Monitoring

Check the **Sync** tab anytime to see:
- When the next automatic sync is scheduled
- History of recent automatic syncs (marked as "Scheduled" type)
- Any failures or warnings

## Email notifications

In **Account Settings > Notifications**, you can enable:
- **Sync failure alerts** — get emailed when a sync fails after all retries
- These are per-user, not per-project

## Costs

Automatic sync is available on all paid plans. The sync interval options are the same across all plans.
