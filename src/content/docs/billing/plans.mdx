## Free plan

The free plan lets you explore the dashboard and set up your account, but you cannot create projects or sync data until you upgrade.

## Starter

Best for small organizations with a single directory. One project, up to 1,000 records.

## Pro

Best for organizations managing multiple directories or larger datasets. Up to 5 projects, 10,000 records per project.

## Agency

For agencies or large organizations managing directories for multiple clients. Unlimited projects, 50,000 records per project. Custom pricing — [contact us](mailto:support@fieldflow.io).

## Trial

All paid plans include a **14-day free trial**. No credit card required to start. You can cancel anytime during the trial with no charge.
