---
title: Managing Your Subscription
description: Upgrade, downgrade, cancel, or update payment info.
---

## View your current plan

Go to **Billing** in the left sidebar. You'll see:
- Your current plan name
- Subscription status (active, trialing, canceled)
- Current billing period end date

## Upgrade your plan

1. Go to **Billing**
2. Click **Upgrade Plan**
3. Select the plan you want
4. Choose monthly or annual billing
5. Complete checkout via Stripe

Annual billing saves roughly 20% compared to monthly.

## Manage your subscription

Click **Manage Billing** to open the Stripe customer portal where you can:
- Update your payment method (credit card)
- Switch between monthly and annual billing
- View and download invoices
- Cancel your subscription

## Cancel your subscription

1. Go to **Billing** > **Manage Billing**
2. Click **Cancel plan** in the Stripe portal
3. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period
4. After that, your organization reverts to the Free plan

Your data is not deleted when you cancel — your projects and records remain but become inaccessible until you resubscribe.

## Payment failures

If a payment fails, you will receive an email notification. Stripe will retry the charge automatically over several days. Update your payment method in the Stripe portal to resolve the issue.

## Who can manage billing?

Only organization **owners** and **admins** can access billing settings. Regular members cannot view or change the subscription.
